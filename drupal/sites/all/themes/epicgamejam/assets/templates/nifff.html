The Epic Game Jam's HQ is in Switzerland, in Necuhâtel. It's precisely at the Case à Chocs that the heart of the events will take place, with about fifty jammers giving all they have to make the epicest of games.

Available seats are sadly limited. However, you are free to organize a room or space wherever you please so that you can get togetheter with other epic jammers. Don't forget to tweet us your location and we'll be sure to get the word around! -> @EpicGameJam

NEW THIS YEAR: NEW LOCATIONS!
There's some awesome people out there, motivated to host the Epic Game Jam between their walls. So, if you can't make it in Neuchatel or you prefer to jam from other place, feel free to notice us! The Epic spirit is everywhere.

MORE INFORMATIONs

COSTS: Free!
FOOD AND DRINKs:

No need to worry about that : you will be fed and hydrated ! We will provide you with free liquids (coffee, Redbull, beer, … water), as well as with free solid food during the whole weekend.

SLEEPING:

With so little time at hand, are you really thinking of sleeping ? We nevertheless have solutions for those who come from afar or are longing for a cat nap. For more information: hello@epicgamejam.com
